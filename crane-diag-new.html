📋
How to Use Crane Diagnostic Agent
5-step guide + pro tips
▼
1
Select Your Crane from the Fleet Panel
Click the correct crane model in the left panel. This loads the manufacturer-specific KB documents (Liebherr, MacGregor, NOV, SeaTrax, Favelle Favco) and sets the diagnostic context automatically.
2
Describe the Fault Symptom Clearly
Type the exact symptom — include the affected circuit (hoist, luff, slew), component model, observed pressure readings, and whether it is intermittent or constant. More detail means a better diagnosis.
3
Follow the 10-State Diagnostic Sequence
The bar at the top tracks 10 diagnostic states — from Safety Isolation to Final Check. The AI advances through each state as you answer. Click any node to jump directly.
4
Monitor Live Gauges While Diagnosing
The fleet panel shows live system pressure, case drain, oil temperature and flow. These values are sent with every AI query, so the diagnosis always reflects current operating conditions.
5
Export Diagnostic Report for Your DPR
When diagnosis is complete, tap "Export Diagnostic Report" in the context panel. This downloads a full report — crane model, live parameters, and the complete conversation — ready for your Daily Progress Report.
💡 Pro Tips
Press Enter to send — Shift+Enter for a new line.
Use the Quick chips under the input box for one-tap fault starters.
KB references like KB-12 appear as teal tags below AI replies.
Ask to "Run full 10-state diagnostic sequence" for a complete structured analysis.
Always quote actual pressure readings — the AI uses them for precise diagnosis.
