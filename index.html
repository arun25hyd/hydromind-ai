Case drain pressure on A10V is reading 4.8 bar during hoisting. Is this a shaft seal issue?
HYDROMIND AI
A case drain of 4.8 bar on the Rexroth A10V exceeds the standard 3 bar threshold and strongly indicates shaft seal degradation or internal bypass. On the BOS 47000 hoisting circuit this is typically caused by:

1. Worn shaft seal (replace per RA 92003)
2. Blocked case drain line (check 1.5 bar back-pressure limit)
3. Elevated case temperature — verify oil temp ≤60°C
