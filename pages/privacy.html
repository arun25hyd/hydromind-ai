Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026  ·  HydroMind.AI  ·  Abu Dhabi, UAE

HydroMind.AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user of this platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect account data (name, email, encrypted password), usage data (AI queries, session activity), and technical data (browser type, IP address via Vercel infrastructure). Chat history is stored locally in your browser only — not on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

To provide the HydroMind.AI platform, manage your account and subscription, send essential service communications, and analyse platform usage to improve features and performance.

3. AI Query Processing

Your AI Advisor queries are processed via the Anthropic Claude API. Anthropic's own Privacy Policy applies to all AI processing. HydroMind.AI does not store AI conversations on its own servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Data is shared only with Anthropic (AI processing), Vercel (hosting infrastructure), and legal authorities if required by applicable UAE law.

5. Data Security

Passwords are encrypted before storage using industry-standard hashing. API keys are stored as encrypted server-side environment variables. All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.

6. Cookies

We use cookies and browser localStorage solely for authentication session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You may clear cookies at any time via your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Contact

For all privacy inquiries:
