FAQ

Common Questions

Can I try before I pay?+
Yes. Register a free account and use the Basic tier with 20 AI queries per month at no cost. Pro and Enterprise plans include a 7-day free trial with no card required.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. No long-term contracts. Cancel from your account dashboard — your plan remains active until the end of the billing period.
How is payment processed?+
Payments are processed securely via Stripe. We accept all major credit and debit cards. Your card details are never stored on our servers.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?+
Yes. Change your plan anytime from account settings. Changes take effect immediately with prorated billing.
Is this suitable for offshore operations?+
Yes. HydroMind.AI is built specifically for offshore crane supervisors and hydraulic technicians. The KB includes Liebherr, Favco, Rexroth, Sauer Danfoss, and MacGregor field cases from real offshore operations.
What OEM manuals are included in Pro?+
Pro includes 48+ indexed OEM manuals across KB1–KB48 covering Rexroth A4VG/A10VO/A10VSO, Sauer Danfoss Series 90/S45, Eaton, Liebherr CBG 350 Litronic, MacGregor HMC, NOV AHC, SeaTrax, Favelle Favco, and more.
