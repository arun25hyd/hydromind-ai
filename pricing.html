Frequently Asked Questions
What counts as a query on the Free plan? +
Each message you send to the AI Advisor counts as one query. The free plan allows 10 queries per day, which resets at midnight UTC. Suggestion card clicks, quick actions, and typed questions all count equally.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription at any time? +
Yes — you can cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees, no lock-in contracts.
How does the annual billing discount work? +
Switching to annual billing gives you 20% off the monthly rate — Pro goes from $29/mo to $23.20/mo (billed as $278.40/year). The saving is applied automatically at checkout when you toggle to Annual.
What hydraulic systems and cranes does HydroMind.AI cover? +
The AI is trained on 223 KB documents covering Liebherr, NOV, MacGregor, SeaTrax, Favelle Favco, Rexroth, Eaton, Danfoss, Sauer, Parker, Sun Hydraulics, and more. It covers offshore cranes, marine deck equipment, industrial hydraulic systems, HPUs, and mobile plant across ISO 4413 and DNV-ST-0378 standards.
Is there a trial period for Pro? +
We offer a 7-day free trial of Pro for new users. Full access to all features — no credit card required to start the trial. You'll be asked for payment details only when the trial ends.
Can the Enterprise plan integrate with our existing CMMS or asset management system? +
Yes — the Enterprise REST API can be integrated with most CMMS platforms. We support webhook events for fault detections and can provide custom API endpoints for your specific workflow. Contact our enterprise team to discuss your integration requirements.
