Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use HydroMind.AI offline on a vessel?
+

HydroMind.AI requires an internet connection for AI queries. However, the Knowledge Base can be accessed in limited read-only mode offline if you've loaded it recently. We're building a fully offline-capable bundle for vessel use — join the waitlist.

What OEM manuals are in the Knowledge Base?
+

The KB covers Rexroth (A4VG, A10VO, A6VM, 4WE valves), Sauer Danfoss (Series 90), Parker (PV series, proportional valves), Eaton Vickers, Sun Hydraulics, Liebherr (CBG 350), MacGregor (HMC), NOV (AHC systems), SeaTrax (S330), Favco (M440D), Siemens (S7-1200 PLC), and Danfoss (FC302 VFD). New entries are added regularly — check the KB version ticker.

Does the AI know about DNV-ST-0378 and ISO standards?
+

Yes. The AI Advisor is specifically trained on offshore hydraulic engineering standards including DNV-ST-0378 (offshore cranes), ISO 1219-1 (hydraulic schematic symbols), ISO 4406 (fluid cleanliness), ISO 6743 (hydraulic fluids), and DNV class notation requirements. It will cite standards in its responses where applicable.

Can Enterprise teams add their own KB entries?
+

Yes. Enterprise plans include custom KB entry creation — you can upload your fleet's specific equipment manuals, inspection records, and maintenance procedures. The AI Advisor will reference these in answers alongside the standard KB. Contact sales for onboarding details.

Is my data secure offshore?
+

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. AI conversations are not used for model training. For Enterprise clients, data residency options (EU / US) are available. We comply with GDPR and do not store sensitive technical documents on our servers — only encrypted session metadata.

How accurate is the AI for hydraulic fault diagnosis?
+

The AI is a powerful aid for experienced technicians — it excels at suggesting diagnostic paths, referencing OEM specs, and explaining hydraulic principles. It is not a replacement for hands-on diagnosis. Always verify AI suggestions against actual measurements, OEM documentation, and your own engineering judgement before making repairs on safety-critical offshore equipment.

Can I switch plans or cancel anytime?
+

Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime before the next billing cycle. Annual plans can be cancelled but are non-refundable after the first 14 days. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings — changes take effect at the next billing cycle.
