Crane Diagnostic Agent
AI-powered hydraulic fault diagnosis for offshore cranes. Select a crane from the fleet panel, then tap a quick-start fault below or type your own symptom.
⚡ Quick-Start Faults — Select to Begin Diagnosis
📋
How to Use Crane Diagnostic Agent
Click to expand — 5-step guide + pro tips
▼
1
Select Your Crane from the Fleet Panel
Click on the correct crane model in the left panel. This loads the manufacturer-specific KB documents (Liebherr, MacGregor, NOV, SeaTrax, Favelle Favco) and sets the diagnostic context automatically.
2
Describe the Fault Symptom Clearly
Type the exact symptom — include the affected circuit (hoist, luff, slew), component model if known, observed pressure readings, and whether it is intermittent or constant. More detail = better diagnosis.
3
Follow the 10-State PLC Diagnostic Sequence
The bar at the top tracks 10 diagnostic states — from Safety Isolation through to Final Check. The AI progresses through each state as you answer follow-up questions. You can click any state node to jump directly.
4
Monitor Live Gauges While Diagnosing
The left panel shows live system pressure, case drain pressure, oil temperature and flow. These values are injected into every AI query automatically — so the AI always knows the current operating conditions.
5
Export Diagnostic Report for Your DPR
Once diagnosis is complete, click "Export Diagnostic Report" in the right panel. This downloads a full text report — crane model, live parameters, and the complete AI conversation — ready to paste into your Daily Progress Report.
💡 Pro Tips
Press Enter to send — Shift+Enter for a new line in your message.
Use the Quick Hint chips below the input box for one-tap common fault starters.
KB references like KB-12 or KB-34 in AI responses are clickable — they appear as teal tags below the message.
Ask the AI to "Run full 10-state diagnostic sequence" to get a comprehensive structured fault analysis.
Always quote actual pressure readings (bar/PSI) from your gauges — the AI uses these for precise diagnosis.
